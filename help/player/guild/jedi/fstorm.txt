 -= Force Storm =-  (Jedi Guild Command)

 Usage: fstorm <room exit>

 The Jedi will attempt to create a storm which bends time and space in an adjacent room.

 A Jedi who uses this will be drawn closer to the Dark side of the Force.

 This command uses the Force Lightning skill (lightning), and is gained with its mastery.

 See also: jedi, dark_jedi, lightning
