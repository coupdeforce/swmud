 -= Heal =-  (Jedi Guild Command)

 Usage: heal [living]

 The Jedi will attempt to heal him or herself, or the specified living being.

 A Jedi who uses this will be drawn closer to the Light side of the Force.

 This command uses the Force Healing skill (healing), and gains the following abilities:

    1 Heal Other's Wounds
    2 Adept Heal
    3 Cure Poison*
    4 Cure Other's Poison*
    5 Adept Cure*
    8 Master Heal
    9 Master Cure*

 * This is not yet coded.

 See also: jedi, light_jedi, healing, healaccel, superheal
