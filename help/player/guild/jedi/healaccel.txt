 -= Healaccel =-  (Jedi Guild Command)

 Usage: healaccel [living]

 The Jedi will attempt to accelerate his or her natural healing rate, or that of the specified living being.

 A Jedi who uses this will be drawn closer to the Light side of the Force.

 This command uses the Force Healing skill (healing), is gained at rank 6, and gains the following abilities:

    7 Accelerate Other's Healing

 See also: jedi, light_jedi, healing, heal, superheal
