 -= Lightning =-  (Jedi Guild Command)

 Usage: lightning [target] [room exit]

 The Jedi will attempt to channel lightning from their arms to the current target or a specified target.

 A Jedi who uses this will be drawn closer to the Dark side of the Force.

 This command uses the Force Lightning skill (lightning), and gains the following abilities:

    1  Focus
    2  Concentration
    3  Chain Lightning
    4  Adept Focus
    5  Adept Concentration
    6  Ranged Lightning
    7  Master Focus
    8  Master Concentration
    9  Divergent Lightning

 See also: jedi, dark_jedi, lightning, fstorm
