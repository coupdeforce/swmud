 -= Superheal =-  (Jedi Guild Command)

 Usage: superheal [living]

 The Jedi will attempt to superheal him or herself, or the specified living being.

 A Jedi who uses this will be drawn closer to the Light side of the Force.

 This command uses the Force Healing skill (healing), and is gained with its mastery.

 See also: jedi, light_jedi, healing, heal, healaccel
